Terms of Use

Published by Fledgewright · Effective date: July 21, 2026

These are the terms you’re agreeing to, in plain language. Please read them before you start.

What this is

UnDistorted is a self-guided program for noticing and challenging distorted thinking. It is educational. It does not diagnose or treat anything, it is not therapy, and it is not a substitute for care from a professional.

What it can bring up

The work asks you to challenge beliefs you may have held for a long time, and that can be uncomfortable. Sometimes it surfaces more than you expected. You set the pace and you can stop at any point.

If you are in danger or you need someone right now, “Get Support Now” is in the menu on every screen, and you can reach it without agreeing to anything.

Your data is yours to keep

Everything you write stays on your device. We run no servers and have no way to see your entries, recover them, or restore them for you.

Your phone’s own backup systems are switched off for this app, so nothing is copied to Google Drive or iCloud. That means the export in Data Management is the only copy that can move to a new phone. Keeping one is up to you, and if you lose the device without one, the work is gone.

What you are agreeing to

  • You are using this program by your own choice and at your own pace.
  • You will not treat what it says as medical or psychological advice.
  • If you need professional help, this app is not a replacement for it.
  • Keeping your own backup of your data is your responsibility.

Changes to these terms

If these terms change in a way that matters, the updated version will be posted with a new effective date, both here and in the app, and the app will ask you to read them again before you continue.
